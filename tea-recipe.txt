1. Clean your kettle
2. Add 0.5 (quantity) (1 cup water for 2 cups of tea) water to the kettle, along with fine tea powder and sugar if needed.
3. Bring the water to a boil, until the colour is a beautiful light brown
4. Add the rest 0.5 (quantity) in milk that is at room temperature.
5. Add ginger, masala or any other ingredients you wish to. (optional)
6. Let the tea rise and then use a strainer to get beautiful and refreshing tea!
